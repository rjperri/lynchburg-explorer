Overview

Distance: 4 mi Time: 2 hrs (2mph) Elevation Gain: 604 ft Difficulty: easy Attractions: woods, solitude, pup-friendly Directions: Google Maps
For a great forest hike nestled deep within Candlers Mountain look for "A Trail Too far" and the "Spirit Trail". When these two trails are connected with Hydaway Lake Rd, one can have a great small escape from everyday busy life. These trails offer stream crossings, solitude, tree views, and some animal watching. Also, this hike will meander through Liberty University's Disc Golf Course, so be sure to bring a Frisbee. The trail begins at the Camp Hydaway Rd trail-head parking lot, and takes about 2 hours to complete.

Guide

Distance (Miles) Description
0.0 Begin the hike by taking the gravel road. This road is Hydaway Lake Rd
You will pass through the Liberty University's Frisbee Golf Course
0.5 After about half mile of walking, turn left onto A Trail Too Far
The trail gradually climbs up Candlers Mountain. You will cross a stream and Hydaway Lake Rd several times
2.0 After 1.5 miles, you should be near Candlers Mountain Rd. And intersect the Goat Path trail. Make a right for a little bit and then you will intersect the Mike Donahue Trail. Make a right on that
Stay on the Mike Donahue trail for about .5 miles till you see a side trail on your right
2.5 Go down this trail till it intersects a dirt road. This is the Hydaway Lake Rd again
2.6 Once at Hydaway Lake Rd intersection, make a left on the rd
2.9 After a short uphill climb, you will come to a fork in the road. Take the right fork up hill.
3.0 In .1 miles you'll reach a small summit, here take the Spirit Trail left
3.5 Continue along the Spirit Trail for about a half mile, crossing Hydaway Lake Rd once. Eventually the Spirit Trail will end by intersecting with Hydaway Lake Rd. Here you will make a left on the road
4.0 Continue along the road till you reach the parking lot, finishing the hike
